How to restore a purchase

Open Time Zone Buddy → Settings → Restore Purchases. Your Lifetime purchase or Plus subscription will be restored automatically using your Apple ID. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If you continue to have trouble, contact support.
